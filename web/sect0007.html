3 Corner Injectivity Theorem

In this section, we show the following theorem, which is crucial for the rest of the argument.

Theorem 3.1

There exists a monotone sofa \(S\) of maximum area with its inner corner path \(\mathbf{x}\colon [0, \pi / 2] \rightarrow \mathbb {R}^2\) injective.

The proof of this theorem is involved, and has computer-assisted steps. 1
